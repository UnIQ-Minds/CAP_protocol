## Troubleshooting

### "This extension is not from the Chrome Web Store"
This warning is normal for manually installed extensions. Your extension will still work.

### Extension disappeared after Chrome update
Chrome may disable unpacked extensions after updates. Just go back to `chrome://extensions` and re-enable it.

### How do I know it's working?
Visit our test page to trigger a simulated attack - you should see a warning popup.
